Sim Racing

I've recently picked up sim racing. Currently, my favorite game is Assetto Corsa. It's Italian for "Race Setup". My favorite car to drive is the Formula 1 Hybrid 2022 car made by Race Sim Studios. To really make the experience even better, I like to jump into the car in Virtual Reality. The immersion you get from sitting inside the car is unbeatable. The added depth perception makes it easier to hit the apex when cornering because you can see how far away you are from the inside line.
